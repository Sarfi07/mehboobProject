The School Uniform - Primary/Montessory Sections

Boys
  • White Shirt with Tie Collar.
  • Grey Shorts / Trousers with two side pockets
  • Black Shoes and White Shocks
  • White Hankerchief
Girls
  • White Shirt with Tie Collar.
  • Grey Shirt
  • Black Shoes and White Shocks
  • White Ribbon / Hair Band
Saturday Only
  • House Uniforms
  • White Skirts (Girls)
  • White Shirts (Boys)
  • White Ribbon / Hair Band
  • White Shoes and white Shocks
  • White hankerchief
Winter(For Primary)

During the winter season (October to March) a red pullover may be used or if anything warm has to be used it may be put on under the blouse or shirts. The uniform cloth, wool for sweater should be purchased from the millshop prescribed by the school for maintaining uniform shade and quality of cloth.

The shirts must be tucked into the shorts.

Note : Uniforms be maintained as prescribed.

The School Uniform - Secondary Sections

Boys
  • White Shirt with Tie Collar
  • Grey Shorts / Trousers with Two Side Pockets
  • Black Shoes and white Shocks
  • Tie and belt as prescribed by the school
  • White Hankerchief
Girls
  • White Blouse/Kurta
  • White Skirts/Salwar
  • Black Shoes and White Shocks
  • White Ribbon / White Hair Band
  • White Hankerchief
Winter(For Secondary)

During the winter season (October to March) a navy blue pullover may be used or if anything warm has to be used it may be put on under the blouse or shirts. The uniform cloth, wool for sweater should be purchased from the millshop prescribed by the school for maintaining uniform shade and quality of cloth.

The shirts must be tucked into the shorts.

Necessary attendance obedience of rules or order.
