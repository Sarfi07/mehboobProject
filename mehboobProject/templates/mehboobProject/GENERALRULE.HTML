GENERAL RULES OF SCHOOL MUST BE FOLLOWED-----

  • Respect: Show respect to teachers, staff members, fellow students, and school property. Treat others as you would like to be treated.
  • Attendance and Punctuality: Attend school regularly and arrive on time. Follow the school's attendance policies and notify the school in case of any absences or delays.
  • Uniform/Dress Code: Follow the school's dress code policy and wear the designated uniform or appropriate attire as instructed.
  • Preparedness: Come to school prepared with the necessary materials such as books, notebooks, pens, and other supplies required for class.
  • Homework and Assignments: Complete homework and assignments on time. Put effort into your work and seek help from teachers when needed.
  • Classroom Etiquette: Follow classroom rules and guidelines established by the teacher. Raise your hand before speaking, be attentive, and actively participate in class activities.
  • Personal Hygiene: Maintain good personal hygiene by practicing cleanliness, including regular handwashing, proper grooming, and wearing clean clothes.
  • Safety: Follow safety rules and guidelines provided by the school. Report any unsafe conditions or incidents to a teacher or staff member immediatel
  • Bullying and Harassment: Treat others with kindness and compassion. Avoid bullying, harassment, or any form of mistreatment. Report any incidents to a trusted adult.
  • Technology Usage: Use technology devices responsibly as per school policies. Do not engage in cyberbullying, access inappropriate content, or misuse digital resources.
  • Respect for Property: Take care of school property, including books, equipment, and facilities. Do not engage in vandalism or theft.
  • Noise and Disturbance: Maintain a quiet and conducive environment for learning. Avoid creating excessive noise or disturbances in classrooms and other common areas
