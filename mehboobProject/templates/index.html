From The Desk Of Principal

Dear Student,

At Rashtra Bhasha Vidyalaya Jorhat, we strive to offer the very best of teaching and learning that has been specially tutored to meet the needs of the youngsters of today. The selection of excellent dedicated staff and a willingness to innovate in educational practiceshave all contributed to the academic success achieved by students at the school and beyond.

The challenge to be the best, to be true citizens of our country is supported by a holistic approach to personal learning. The classroom programmes are rich and engaging and the range of cultural and sporting activities provide a number of opportunities that allow every student access to an environment where they can participate and excel.

In all our endeavors at Rashtrabhasha Vidyalaya Jorhat, we are supported by the School Management Committee. We, at Rashtrabhasha Vidyalaya Jorhat view that effective schooling is a three way dynamic, with the student, family and the School working together to contribute to the learning process. Our goal is to give our students the necessary tools to have a choice about their future.

" Education is the key to personal and societal growth. It plays a crucial role in shaping individuals and preparing them for future challenges."

"Education is not limited to classrooms; it is a lifelong process of learning and acquiring knowledge from various sources and experiences."

"Education fosters social harmony and understanding by promoting tolerance, empathy, and respect for diverse cultures and perspectives."

With Best Wishes
Principal,
Rashtra Bhasha Vidyalaya Jorhat
